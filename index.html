How to play the game:

A randomized "code" of four colors (including duplicates) has been generated. You have 12 tries to guess the secret code by selecting a combination of colors from the box on the furthest left.

Clicking a color will add the color to your guess. You can edit your guess by deleting a color or clearing the guess entirely.

Once you submit your guess, the mini-board to the right of your guess will display how accurate it was. A yellow circle means your guess contains one of the correct colors but in an incorrect position. A green circle means your guess contains one of the correct colors in the correct position. The mini-board does not confirm the order of your guess.

Four green circles means you've won the game!
